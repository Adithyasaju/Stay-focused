Take Regular Breaks

The Power of Pausing

Working without breaks leads to fatigue and reduced mental sharpness. Regular breaks refresh the brain, reduce eye strain, and improve overall work quality.

Best Break Strategies

  • Use the 50/10 rule: work 50 minutes, break 10
  • Stretch, take a short walk, or do deep breathing
  • Avoid screens during break time
  • Drink water or grab a healthy snack

Breaks are not wasted time—they're fuel for better work.
