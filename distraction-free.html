Build Your Distraction-Free Zone

Why It Matters

Creating a distraction-free environment is key to deep work and mental clarity. Our brains can only focus on one thing at a time, and removing digital and physical clutter can dramatically improve performance.

Tips to Set It Up

  • Use a dedicated workspace (not your bed!)
  • Keep your desk clean and minimal
  • Use noise-cancelling headphones or calming background music
  • Put your phone on airplane mode or in another room
  • Use site blockers like Cold Turkey or LeechBlock
